# Sacred Portal Wellness
## Client Website Plan

### Project Summary
We are building your new website at **sacredportalwellness.com**.

This site is designed to:
- clearly show your services and products
- help local clients in San Diego find you
- make checkout simple and secure
- reflect your brand style and voice

### What You Will Receive
- A professional `.com` website that you own
- A full online shop connected to your existing Square products
- Secure checkout (cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Two business email addresses: `business@sacredportalwellness.com` and `info@sacredportalwellness.com`
- A complete coaching page for The Sacred Portal Path on your website
- A Google Business Profile setup focused on local visibility
- SEO setup so people can find your services online

### Phase 1: Discovery and Content Collection
We gather and organize everything before design and build start.

What happens:
- Collect current website text, photos, and product details
- Collect logo files, coaching photos, and testimonial content
- Confirm your current social links and brand details
- Organize content into a clean build-ready folder system

### Phase 2: Domain, Email, and Account Setup
We set up your core online foundation.

What happens:
- Register and configure `sacredportalwellness.com`
- Set up both Gmail-based business inboxes under your domain
- Set up Google Business Profile as a service-area business (your home address stays private)
- Connect Square so your website can show products and accept payments

### Phase 3: Brand and Design Direction
We build your visual direction before development begins.

Brand direction:
- Colors: forest green, white, black
- Style: earthy, grounded, feminine, mystical with clinical trust
- Visual accents: moss/forest/vines/sacred geometry, used lightly

What happens:
- Create design mockups for key pages
- Review and approve visual direction with you before build

### Phase 4: Website Pages and Copy
We finalize your full site structure and page content.

Core pages:
- Home
- Shop
- The Sacred Portal Path
- About the Creator
- Testimonials
- Workshops and Retreats (Launching Soon)
- Contact

Search and trust pages:
- What Is Yoni Steaming?
- FAQ
- San Diego Wellness Services
- Blog
- Disclaimer
- Return and Exchange Policy

What happens:
- Move over and improve existing content
- Write clear, client-friendly copy with your voice
- Make sure credentials and trust signals are visible across the site

### Phase 5: Website Build and Shopping Experience
We build the live website and connect your product and checkout flow.

What happens:
- Build responsive pages for mobile and desktop
- Sync products from Square so updates stay centralized
- Build cart and checkout with secure payment handling
- Add shipping and local delivery rules:
  - Shipping available
  - Local San Diego delivery available
  - Free local delivery over $100
- Add clear product-level return policy messaging

### Phase 6: SEO and Local Search Setup
We set up your search visibility so the right clients can find you.

What happens:
- Optimize your Google Business Profile
- Set up search basics on every page (page titles, descriptions, and clean structure)
- Add search-friendly business details for products, services, FAQ, and reviews
- Apply keyword strategy by priority:
  - Quick wins first (local and lower competition terms)
  - Medium competition targets next
  - Long-term content terms through blog growth

### Phase 7: Testing and Quality Review
We test everything before launch.

What happens:
- Test every page on phone, tablet, and desktop
- Test full checkout flow and delivery logic
- Test site speed and usability
- Review search setup and links
- Complete final content review with you before launch approval

### Phase 8: Launch and Handoff
We launch the new site and transfer day-to-day management to you.

What happens:
- Connect domain to the new website
- Run live launch checks and monitor first-week performance
- Set up analytics and search tracking
- Provide clear handoff documentation for:
  - Product updates
  - Blog publishing
  - Review response workflow

### Phase 9: Security and Final Protection Check
We complete a final security pass before closeout.

What happens:
- Verify secure settings across domain, email, and website
- Verify checkout protection
- Run security checks for known software risks
- Confirm backup and recovery process
- Document ongoing monthly security checks
