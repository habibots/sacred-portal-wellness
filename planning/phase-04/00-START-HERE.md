## 🎯 PHASE 4 OVERVIEW

### What is Phase 4?

Phase 4 transforms design specifications and brand assets into **production-ready content** for every page of the Sacred Portal Wellness website. This phase bridges the gap between design (Phase 3) and development (Phase 5).

### Why This Phase is Critical

**Without Phase 4:**
- Developers build with placeholder "Lorem ipsum" text
- SEO opportunities are missed during development
- Brand voice becomes inconsistent across pages
- Trust signals (credentials, disclaimers) are incomplete
- Launch delays occur during last-minute content revisions
- Metadata and internal linking are afterthoughts

**With Phase 4:**
- Every page has final, approved copy before development
- SEO is built into content from the start
- Brand voice is consistent and professional
- Compliance requirements are met
- Development can proceed without content blockers
- Launch happens on schedule

## 📝 CONTENT PRODUCTION PROCESS

### Step 1: Review Phase 1-3 Assets

**Before writing, review:**
- Phase 1 brand voice guidelines
- Phase 1 existing content
- Phase 3 component content limits
- Phase 3 page layout specifications

### Step 2: Create Content Brief

**For each page, document:**
- Page purpose and goal
- Target audience
- Primary keyword
- Required sections
- CTAs needed
- Trust signals needed
- Character limits (from design system)

### Step 3: Write First Draft

**Follow guidelines:**
- Use brand voice (warm, professional, empowering)
- Integrate keywords naturally
- Stay within character limits
- Include all required sections
- Add clear CTAs
- Embed credentials where appropriate

### Step 4: Self-Review

**Check:**
- Brand voice consistency
- Keyword integration
- Character limits
- All required sections present
- CTAs clear and compelling
- No unsupported health claims
- Grammar and spelling

### Step 5: Peer Review

**Get feedback on:**
- Content accuracy
- Brand voice alignment
- SEO optimization
- Compliance issues

### Step 6: Compliance Review

**Verify:**
- Health claims are evidence-based
- Disclaimers are present
- Credentials are accurate
- Policy language is clear

### Step 7: Stakeholder Review

**Submit for:**
- Content approval
- Brand voice validation
- Final edits

### Step 8: Finalize & Package

**Prepare for development:**
- Incorporate all feedback
- Final proofread
- Format for implementation
- Add to final content package

## ✅ QUALITY STANDARDS

### Brand Voice Requirements

**Tone Attributes:**
- ✅ Warm but professional
- ✅ Educational without being preachy
- ✅ Empowering
- ✅ Authentic
- ✅ Earthy, grounded, feminine

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Overly clinical language
- ❌ Condescending tone
- ❌ Vague mystical claims
- ❌ Aggressive sales language
- ❌ Medical jargon without explanation

### SEO Requirements

**Every page must have:**
- ✅ Primary keyword in H1
- ✅ Keywords in first paragraph
- ✅ Natural keyword integration (no stuffing)
- ✅ Descriptive headings (H2, H3)
- ✅ Internal links to related pages
- ✅ Unique meta description

### Accessibility Requirements

**Content must be:**
- ✅ Scannable (short paragraphs, bullet points)
- ✅ Clear heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- ✅ Descriptive link text (no "click here")
- ✅ Simple language (8th grade reading level)
- ✅ Alt text for all images (added in Phase 5)

### Compliance Requirements

**Health & Wellness Standards:**
- ✅ No absolute medical claims
- ✅ Evidence-based language ("may help" vs "cures")
- ✅ Credentials displayed: RN, BSN, HC-NC
- ✅ Disclaimers on coaching and product pages
- ✅ Clear return/exchange policies

### Technical Requirements

**Content Format:**
- ✅ Markdown format (.md files)
- ✅ Structured headings
- ✅ Character limits respected
- ✅ Placeholder for images noted
- ✅ CTA placement documented
