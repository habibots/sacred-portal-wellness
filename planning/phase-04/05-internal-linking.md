## 🎯 OVERVIEW

### Purpose

Internal linking connects pages within the Sacred Portal Wellness website, improving:
- **SEO:** Helps search engines discover and understand page relationships
- **User Experience:** Guides visitors to relevant content
- **Conversion:** Directs users toward key actions (shop, book coaching)
- **Authority:** Distributes page authority throughout the site

### Internal Linking Goals

1. **No Orphan Pages** - Every page is linked from at least 3 other pages
2. **Clear Hierarchy** - Important pages receive more internal links
3. **Contextual Relevance** - Links are naturally integrated into content
4. **Descriptive Anchors** - Link text describes destination page
5. **Strategic Flow** - Guide users toward conversion actions

## 📐 INTERNAL LINKING PRINCIPLES

### 1. Link Hierarchy

**Priority 1 (Most Links):**
- Homepage
- Shop / All Products
- The Sacred Portal Path (Coaching)
- What is Yoni Steaming? (SEO pillar)

**Priority 2 (Moderate Links):**
- About / Meet the Creator
- Product Category Pages
- FAQ
- Contact

**Priority 3 (Fewer Links):**
- Testimonials
- Individual Product Pages
- Policy Pages
- Blog Posts (future)

### 2. Anchor Text Best Practices

**Good Anchor Text:**
- ✅ "The Sacred Portal Path coaching program"
- ✅ "Learn about yoni steaming"
- ✅ "Shop organic yoni steam blends"
- ✅ "Meet the creator, Amber, RN, BSN, HC-NC"

**Poor Anchor Text:**
- ❌ "Click here"
- ❌ "Read more"
- ❌ "This page"
- ❌ "Link"

**Keyword-Rich (Use Sparingly):**
- "holistic wellness coaching"
- "yoni steaming San Diego"
- "women's health coach"

### 3. Link Placement

**High-Value Locations:**
1. **Main Content** - Contextual links within body text
2. **Navigation** - Header and footer menus
3. **Sidebar/Related** - Related content sections
4. **CTAs** - Call-to-action buttons and links

**Link Density:**
- 3-5 contextual links per 500 words
- Not too many (looks spammy)
- Not too few (missed opportunities)

### 4. Link Types

**Contextual Links:**
- Natural links within content
- Most valuable for SEO
- Example: "If you're new to yoni steaming, read our complete guide."

**Navigation Links:**
- Header menu
- Footer menu
- Breadcrumbs

**Related Content Links:**
- "You may also like"
- "Related products"
- "Read next"

**CTA Links:**
- Buttons and prominent links
- Drive to conversion actions
- Example: "Book Your Free Discovery Call"

## 🔍 LINK AUDIT

### Orphan Page Check

**Definition:** Pages with fewer than 3 internal links pointing to them

**Audit Process:**
1. List all pages
2. Count incoming links for each page
3. Identify pages with < 3 links
4. Add contextual links to orphan pages

**Target:** 0 orphan pages

### Link Distribution Check

**Audit Questions:**
- Do priority pages receive the most links?
- Are links distributed evenly across the site?
- Are there any broken internal links?
- Are anchor texts descriptive and varied?

### Link Mapping Spreadsheet

**File:** `assets/internal-links.csv`

**Columns:**
1. Source Page
2. Destination Page
3. Anchor Text
4. Link Type (Contextual, Navigation, CTA, Related)
5. Link Location (Header, Body, Footer, Sidebar)
6. Priority (High, Medium, Low)
7. Status (Active, Planned, Broken)

**Example Row:**
```csv
Homepage,The Sacred Portal Path (Coaching),"Explore The Sacred Portal Path",CTA,Body,High,Active
Homepage,Shop / All Products,"Shop Yoni Steam Blends",CTA,Body,High,Active
What is Yoni Steaming?,Shop / Yoni Steam Blends,"Shop organic yoni steam blends",Contextual,Body,High,Active
```
