## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long are the coaching sessions?

Sessions are typically 60 minutes, giving us time to dive deep into your progress and adjust your plan as needed.

### What if I miss a session?

Life happens! We can reschedule sessions with 24-hour notice. The program is designed to be flexible while maintaining momentum.

### Do you take insurance?

Holistic coaching is not typically covered by insurance. This is an investment in your wellness that you pay out-of-pocket. Payment plans may be available.

### What's the refund policy?

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Let's discuss your specific situation if you have concerns.

### Can I do this if I'm not in San Diego?

Yes! All sessions are virtual, so you can participate from anywhere in the United States.

### Do I need to buy special products or supplements?

I'll make recommendations based on your needs, but there's no requirement to purchase specific products. Any herbal or supplement recommendations will be optional and tailored to your budget.

### Is this a substitute for medical care?

No. Holistic coaching complements but does not replace medical care. I encourage you to work with your healthcare provider and will never ask you to stop prescribed treatments.

### How is this different from therapy?

Coaching focuses on wellness goals, lifestyle changes, and moving forward. It's not therapy or mental health treatment. If you need mental health support, I can help you find appropriate resources.
