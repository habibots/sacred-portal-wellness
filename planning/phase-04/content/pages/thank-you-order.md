## What Happens Next

### 1. Order Confirmation Email

You'll receive an email confirmation shortly with your order details. Please check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.

### 2. Order Processing

We'll prepare your order within **2-3 business days**. Each herbal blend is prepared with care and intention.

### 3. Shipping Notification

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with:
- Tracking number
- Estimated delivery date
- Link to track your package

### 4. Delivery

**Standard Shipping:** 3-7 business days after shipment  
**Local Delivery (San Diego):** 2-3 business days after order
