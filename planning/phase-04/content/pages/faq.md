## About Yoni Steaming

### What is yoni steaming?

Yoni steaming, also known as vaginal steaming, is an ancient wellness practice where women sit over a pot of herbal-infused steam. The gentle warmth and herbal properties may support women's wellness, particularly around menstruation, postpartum recovery, and reproductive health.

**[Learn more about yoni steaming](/yoni-steaming)**

### Is yoni steaming safe?

When done correctly with appropriate precautions, yoni steaming can be safe for many women. However, it's not appropriate for everyone. Always consult your healthcare provider before beginning, especially if you're pregnant, have an IUD, or have any medical conditions.

**Do not yoni steam if you:**
- Are pregnant or might be pregnant
- Are currently menstruating
- Have an IUD
- Have an active infection
- Have open wounds or sores

### How often should I yoni steam?

Frequency depends on your individual needs:
- **General wellness:** 1-2 times per month
- **Menstrual support:** 1-2 times between periods (not during)
- **Specific concerns:** Work with a practitioner for personalized guidance

Start conservatively and adjust based on how your body responds.

### When should I NOT yoni steam?

Do not yoni steam if you are:
- Pregnant or trying to conceive
- Currently menstruating
- Have an IUD
- Have an active infection (yeast, bacterial, or STI)
- Have open wounds or sores in the vaginal area
- Experiencing heavy bleeding or spotting

### What herbs are used in yoni steaming?

Common herbs include lavender, rose petals, calendula, mugwort, rosemary, and chamomile. Each herb has different properties - some are calming, some support circulation, and some are traditionally used for cycle support.

All Sacred Portal yoni steam blends use organic, sustainably sourced herbs.

### Can I yoni steam while pregnant?

No. Do not yoni steam if you are pregnant or think you might be pregnant. The heat and herbs could potentially affect pregnancy.

### Can I yoni steam with an IUD?

No. Do not yoni steam if you have an IUD. The heat and herbs could potentially affect the device.

### Does yoni steaming help with fertility?

Yoni steaming is not a fertility treatment. While some women include it as part of their preconception wellness routine, there is no scientific evidence that it improves fertility. Consult a fertility specialist for evidence-based fertility support.

## About Products

### What's in the yoni steam blends?

Each blend contains a unique combination of organic herbs selected for specific intentions. All ingredients are listed on the product page and packaging.

Common ingredients include:
- Lavender (calming)
- Rose petals (nurturing)
- Calendula (soothing)
- Mugwort (cycle support)
- Rosemary (circulation)
- Chamomile (anti-inflammatory)

### Are the ingredients organic?

Yes! All Sacred Portal yoni steam blends use organic, pesticide-free herbs from trusted, sustainable sources.

### How long does a yoni steam blend last?

Each blend typically provides 4-6 steaming sessions, depending on how much you use per session. Store in a cool, dry place in an airtight container to maintain freshness.

### How do I store the blends?

Store yoni steam blends in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep in an airtight container to preserve freshness and potency. Properly stored, blends maintain quality for 6-12 months.

### Can I make my own herbal blend?

Yes, but be sure to use organic, pesticide-free herbs and research which herbs are appropriate for yoni steaming. Some herbs are not safe for this practice. If you're unsure, our pre-made blends are carefully formulated for safety and effectiveness.

### Do you offer refunds on opened products?

For health and safety reasons, opened herbal products cannot be returned or exchanged. Unopened products in original packaging can be returned within 30 days.
