# Meet the Creator

## Hello, I'm Amber

**RN, BSN, HC-NC**

I've been part of the caregiving world since the age of 18. My journey has taken me from traditional hospital settings to creating Sacred Portal Wellness - a space where nursing expertise meets holistic wisdom, and where women can find the support they need to truly thrive.

## My Background

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Nursing with certifications in herbal first aid and holistic nurse coaching. Currently, I practice medicine as a nurse in neurology, and I've spent the last decade working in cardiac, neurology, and emergency medicine.

My education includes:
- Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN)
- Registered Nurse (RN) license
- Board-Certified Holistic Nurse Coach (HC-NC)
- Herbalista School graduate
- State certification for holistic nursing coaching
- Continuing education in herbalism and natural medicine

My life goal is to become a family nurse practitioner specializing in herbal medicine. My passion has always been learning how to bring integrity to my work and make a positive impact on those entrusted to my care.

## The Story of Me

With a nursing background in cardiac, neurology, and emergency medicine, I've spent much of my time ingrained and trained in the traditional healthcare system. There has always been this sense of inescapable sickness among the patients I care for - an energy that never really had to be spoken but was always present: "How did my body get to this point? This isn't what I wanted for myself."

I've found the sick care model is a deeply rooted problem with a complicated way out.

As time moves forward, I've sat closely with how I'm living my life's purpose. I've learned that my heart is always with nursing, but my spirit isn't with the traditional medicine framework. I've learned that my purpose is to no longer engage with a faceless system but to make a real difference in 1:1 interactions with my clients instead.

I know that the care I give - infused through my knowledge and given from human to human - is what truly makes a difference. I've really had to get clear with how I wanted to express my gifts as a healer.

So what and where within the giving sphere can I call home? Naturally, the pivot has been to herbalism. Remember making mud pies in the front yard as a child? It's been something that has felt gentle, intentional, accessible, familiar.

## My Transformation

In the last seven years, I've dealt with the same issues many of my clients face - through trial and error. I was 60 pounds overweight in my early 20s and felt I had unlived potential but didn't know the tools to properly take care of myself in mind, body, and spirit.

I wish I had someone who understood my needs and could both knowledgeably and intuitively guide me to help me overhaul my life in a way that didn't feel overwhelming but was fully effective.

Time and energy aren't something we get back in this life. That's why I created The Sacred Portal Path - to be the woman who walks alongside you in your journey of self-empowerment, self-love, and self-actualization to be the fullest version of yourself that you know you are.

## How I Work

My approach blends evidence-based nursing knowledge with holistic healing practices. I believe in:

- **Personalized Care:** Every woman's journey is unique. I create customized wellness plans that honor your individual needs and goals.

- **Root Cause Focus:** Rather than just managing symptoms, we work together to address the underlying imbalances affecting your health.

- **Natural Medicine:** I incorporate herbal medicine, nutrition, and lifestyle practices that support your body's innate healing wisdom.

- **Empowerment:** My goal is to give you the knowledge and tools to take charge of your own wellness journey.

- **Compassionate Support:** I bring both professional expertise and genuine care to every interaction.

## The Sacred Portal Vision

My purpose in this life is to support my community in true health. To give my gift of knowledge in medicine - lovingly and healthfully - from my soul. The coaching and products of Sacred Portal Wellness are created with this intention in mind.

This is matriarchal medicine for modern women. A space where you can find both the clinical expertise of nursing and the gentle wisdom of holistic healing. Where you're not just a patient, but a whole person deserving of comprehensive, compassionate care.
