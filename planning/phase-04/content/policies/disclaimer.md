## Yoni Steaming Safety

Yoni steaming is not appropriate for everyone. **Do not yoni steam if you:**

- Are pregnant or think you might be pregnant
- Are currently menstruating
- Have an IUD (intrauterine device)
- Have an active infection (yeast, bacterial, or STI)
- Have open wounds or sores in the vaginal area
- Are experiencing heavy bleeding or spotting

Always consult your healthcare provider before beginning yoni steaming, especially if you have any medical conditions.
