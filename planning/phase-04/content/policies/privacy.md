# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 12, 2026  
**Effective Date:** March 12, 2026

Sacred Portal Wellness ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

- **Place an order** - Name, email, shipping address, billing address, phone number
- **Create an account** - Email, password, name
- **Contact us** - Name, email, message content, phone number (if provided)
- **Sign up for our newsletter** - Email address, name (optional)
- **Book coaching services** - Name, email, phone number, health information (if shared)
- **Leave a testimonial** - Name, age, location (if provided), testimonial content

### Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information** - Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Usage Data** - Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked
- **IP Address** - Your internet protocol address
- **Cookies** - Small data files stored on your device (see Cookies section below)

### Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

- **Payment Processors** - Square (transaction information)
- **Shipping Carriers** - USPS, UPS (delivery information)
- **Analytics Providers** - Google Analytics (website usage data)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### Provide Services

- Process and fulfill orders
- Provide coaching services
- Send order confirmations and shipping updates
- Respond to your inquiries and customer service requests
- Manage your account

### Communicate with You

- Send newsletters and marketing communications (with your consent)
- Notify you about new products, services, or promotions
- Send important updates about our policies or services

### Improve Our Services

- Analyze website usage to improve user experience
- Understand customer preferences and trends
- Develop new products and services
- Conduct research and analysis

### Legal and Security

- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and unauthorized transactions
- Protect our rights and property
- Enforce our terms and conditions

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

### Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business:

- **Payment Processing** - Square (to process payments)
- **Shipping** - USPS, UPS (to deliver orders)
- **Email Services** - Email service provider (to send newsletters)
- **Website Hosting** - Hosting provider (to maintain our website)
- **Analytics** - Google Analytics (to analyze website usage)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

- Court orders or legal processes
- Government or regulatory requests
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety
- Investigation of fraud or security issues

### Business Transfers

If Sacred Portal Wellness is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Update

You have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Update or correct inaccurate information
- Request a copy of your information

**To exercise these rights:** Email us at info@sacredportalwellness.com

### Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails by:

- Clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email
- Emailing us at info@sacredportalwellness.com
- Updating your account preferences

**Note:** You will still receive transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates) even if you opt out of marketing.

### Delete Your Information

You can request deletion of your personal information by emailing info@sacredportalwellness.com.

**Note:** We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., order history for tax purposes).

### Do Not Track

Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience.

### Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies** - Required for website functionality (shopping cart, checkout)

**Analytics Cookies** - Help us understand how visitors use our website (Google Analytics)

**Marketing Cookies** - Track your browsing to show relevant ads (if applicable)

### Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Block all cookies** - May affect website functionality
- **Delete cookies** - Remove existing cookies from your device
- **Block third-party cookies** - Allow only our cookies

**To disable Google Analytics:** Visit [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption** - SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- **Secure Servers** - Secure hosting with regular backups
- **Access Controls** - Limited access to personal information
- **Payment Security** - PCI-compliant payment processing through Square

**However:** No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

**Typical Retention Periods:**
- **Order Information** - 7 years (tax and legal requirements)
- **Account Information** - Until you request deletion
- **Marketing Data** - Until you opt out
- **Analytics Data** - 26 months (Google Analytics default)

## Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at info@sacredportalwellness.com.

## Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know** - What personal information we collect and how we use it
- **Right to Delete** - Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out** - Opt out of sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination** - Equal service regardless of privacy choices

**To exercise these rights:** Email info@sacredportalwellness.com

## International Users

Our website is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

By using our website, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

**Material changes** will be communicated via:
- Email notification (if you have an account)
- Prominent notice on our website

Your continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

**Email:** info@sacredportalwellness.com  
**Response Time:** 24-48 hours (Monday-Friday)
