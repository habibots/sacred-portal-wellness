# Shipping Policy

**Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

Sacred Portal Wellness is committed to getting your wellness products to you safely and efficiently. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping options and policies.

## Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping (Nationwide)

**Available:** All 50 United States  
**Carrier:** USPS or UPS (we select based on fastest delivery)  
**Cost:** Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination  
**Estimated Delivery:** 3-7 business days after shipment

### Local Delivery (San Diego Area)

**Available:** Select San Diego ZIP codes  
**Delivery Fee:** $10  
**FREE Delivery:** Orders over $100  
**Estimated Delivery:** 2-3 business days after order

**Eligible ZIP Codes:**
- 92101-92199 (San Diego proper)
- Additional ZIP codes may be available - check at checkout

**Local Delivery Benefits:**
- Faster delivery
- No shipping carrier delays
- Free for orders over $100
- Eco-friendly (reduced packaging)

### Free Shipping Threshold

**Free Standard Shipping:** Orders over $75  
**Free Local Delivery:** Orders over $100

## Processing Time

### Order Processing

**Standard Processing:** 2-3 business days  
**Peak Season:** 3-5 business days (holidays)

Orders are processed Monday-Friday (excluding holidays). Orders placed after 2pm PST will be processed the next business day.

### What "Processing" Means

During processing, we:
- Prepare your order
- Blend fresh herbal products (if applicable)
- Package items securely
- Generate shipping label
- Hand off to carrier

You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

## Tracking Your Order

### Tracking Information

All orders include tracking information sent to your email.

**What You'll Receive:**
- Shipping confirmation email
- Tracking number
- Estimated delivery date
- Link to track package

**Tracking Updates:**
- First scan: Package picked up by carrier
- In transit: Package moving toward destination
- Out for delivery: Package on delivery truck
- Delivered: Package delivered to address

### Tracking Issues

If tracking hasn't updated in 48 hours, contact us at info@sacredportalwellness.com.

## Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on:
- Package weight
- Destination ZIP code
- Shipping method selected

**Typical Shipping Costs:**
- Small orders (under 1 lb): $5-8
- Medium orders (1-3 lbs): $8-12
- Large orders (3+ lbs): $12-18

**Remember:** Free shipping on orders over $75!

## Delivery Details

### Delivery Address

Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate:
- Street address (no PO boxes for local delivery)
- Apartment/unit number (if applicable)
- City, State, ZIP code
- Phone number (for delivery questions)

**We cannot ship to:**
- PO boxes (for local delivery)
- International addresses
- Military APO/FPO addresses (coming soon)

### Delivery Signature

Standard orders do not require signature. Package will be left at delivery address.

**Signature Available Upon Request:**
- Contact us before ordering
- Additional fee may apply
- Recommended for high-value orders

### Safe Delivery

We package all products securely to ensure safe delivery. If your package arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with photos.

## Shipping Restrictions

### What We Ship

- Yoni steam blends
- Yoni seats
- Wellness products
- Gift sets

### What We Don't Ship

Currently, we only ship within the United States. International shipping is not available at this time.

## Holidays & Peak Seasons

### Holiday Shipping

During peak seasons (holidays, sales events):
- Processing time may extend to 3-5 business days
- Carrier delays may occur
- Order early to ensure timely delivery

**Holiday Cutoff Dates:**
We'll post holiday shipping cutoff dates on our website and social media.

### Weather Delays

Severe weather may cause carrier delays beyond our control. We appreciate your patience during these times.

## Lost or Delayed Packages

### If Your Package is Delayed

1. Check tracking for updates
2. Wait 2 business days past estimated delivery
3. Contact carrier with tracking number
4. Contact us if issue persists

### If Your Package is Lost

If tracking shows package as lost or undeliverable:
1. Contact us at info@sacredportalwellness.com
2. Provide order number and tracking information
3. We'll file a claim with carrier
4. We'll reship or refund once claim is resolved

**Note:** Please allow carrier 7-10 business days to locate package before filing claim.

## Address Changes

### Before Shipment

Contact us immediately at info@sacredportalwellness.com to update your address. We'll do our best to accommodate if order hasn't shipped.

### After Shipment

Once shipped, we cannot change the delivery address. Contact the carrier directly with your tracking number to request address change or package hold.

## Packaging

### Eco-Friendly Packaging

We use sustainable packaging materials whenever possible:
- Recyclable boxes
- Biodegradable packing materials
- Minimal plastic use
- Reusable containers when appropriate

### Product Protection

All products are packaged securely to prevent damage during shipping:
- Herbal blends in sealed, protective packaging
- Fragile items wrapped individually
- Cushioning materials as needed

## International Shipping

**Currently Not Available**

We currently ship within the United States only. We hope to offer international shipping in the future.

**Interested in International Shipping?**  
Email info@sacredportalwellness.com to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

## Questions?

Have questions about shipping? We're here to help!

**Contact Us:**
- Email: info@sacredportalwellness.com
- Response time: 24-48 hours (Monday-Friday)
