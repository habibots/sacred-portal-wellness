# Return & Exchange Policy

**Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

We want you to love your Sacred Portal Wellness purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help.

## Product Returns

### 30-Day Return Window

Unopened products in original condition may be returned within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

**To be eligible for a return:**
- Product must be unopened and unused
- Product must be in original packaging
- Return must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
- Proof of purchase required

### Opened Herbal Products

For health and safety reasons, **opened herbal products cannot be returned or exchanged**. This includes:
- Yoni steam blends (once opened)
- Any herbal products that have been used

We appreciate your understanding of this policy, which protects the health and safety of all our customers.

### Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
- Opened herbal products
- Products damaged due to misuse
- Products purchased from third-party retailers
- Sale or clearance items (unless defective)

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product, we'll make it right.

### What to Do

1. **Contact us within 7 days** of receiving the item
2. **Email:** info@sacredportalwellness.com
3. **Include:**
   - Order number
   - Photos of the damaged/defective item
   - Description of the issue

### Our Response

We will:
- Replace the item at no charge, or
- Issue a full refund (including original shipping)
- Cover return shipping costs for damaged/defective items

## How to Initiate a Return

### Step 1: Contact Us

Email **info@sacredportalwellness.com** with:
- Your order number
- Item(s) you wish to return
- Reason for return
- Whether you prefer refund or exchange

### Step 2: Receive Return Authorization

We'll respond within 24-48 hours with:
- Return authorization
- Return shipping address
- Return instructions

### Step 3: Ship Your Return

- Pack item securely in original packaging
- Include copy of order confirmation
- Ship to provided return address
- **Note:** Customer is responsible for return shipping costs (unless item is damaged/defective)

**We recommend using a trackable shipping method.** Sacred Portal Wellness is not responsible for items lost in return transit.

### Step 4: Receive Your Refund or Exchange

Once we receive and inspect your return:
- **Refunds:** Processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method
- **Exchanges:** New item ships within 2-3 business days

## Refund Details

### What Gets Refunded

- Product cost
- Original shipping (if item was damaged/defective)

### What Doesn't Get Refunded

- Return shipping costs (unless item was damaged/defective)
- Original shipping (for customer-initiated returns)

### Refund Timeline

- Returns processed within 5-7 business days of receipt
- Refund appears in your account within 5-10 business days (depending on your bank)

## Exchanges

Prefer to exchange your item? We're happy to help!

**Exchange Process:**
1. Follow return process above
2. Indicate you'd like an exchange
3. Specify which item you'd like instead
4. We'll ship your exchange once we receive your return

**Note:** If the exchange item costs more, you'll be charged the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference.

## Coaching Services

### Coaching Program Refunds

Refunds for The Sacred Portal Path coaching program are handled on a **case-by-case basis**.

**Please contact us to discuss:**
- Email: business@sacredportalwellness.com
- We'll work with you to find a fair resolution

**General Policy:**
- No refunds after 3 sessions have been completed
- Unused sessions may be eligible for partial refund
- Each situation is evaluated individually

## Lost or Stolen Packages

### Tracking Your Order

All orders include tracking information. Please check tracking before contacting us about a lost package.

### If Your Package Shows Delivered But You Didn't Receive It

1. Check with household members
2. Check all possible delivery locations (porch, mailbox, garage)
3. Check with neighbors
4. Contact your local post office with tracking number

If package cannot be located after these steps, contact us at info@sacredportalwellness.com and we'll work with you to resolve the issue.

**Note:** Sacred Portal Wellness is not responsible for packages stolen after confirmed delivery. We recommend shipping to a secure location or using signature confirmation for valuable orders.

## Order Cancellations

### Before Shipping

Orders can be cancelled before shipping at no charge. Contact us immediately at info@sacredportalwellness.com.

### After Shipping

Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You may return the item following our standard return policy once received.

## International Orders

Currently, Sacred Portal Wellness ships within the United States only. International shipping is not available at this time.

## Questions?

If you have questions about returns, exchanges, or your specific situation, we're here to help.

**Contact Us:**
- Email: info@sacredportalwellness.com
- Response time: 24-48 hours (Monday-Friday)
