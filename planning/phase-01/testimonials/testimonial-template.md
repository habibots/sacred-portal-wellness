## Testimonial Checklist

Before adding to website:
- [ ] Written approval obtained and documented
- [ ] Medical claims reviewed and verified/edited
- [ ] Client name/initial format confirmed
- [ ] All three length variants created
- [ ] Spelling and grammar checked
- [ ] Tone aligns with brand voice
- [ ] File saved with naming convention: `YYYY-MM-DD_client-initials_testimonial.md`
