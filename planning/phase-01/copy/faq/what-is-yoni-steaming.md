## Yoni Steam

*Aka vaginal steaming, v-steaming*

An ancient, worldwide practice of womb and feminine connection that utilizes medicinal herbs steamed in hot water to permeate the tissues of the vagina

---

## Why steam?

Certain herbs carry medicinal properties. We can use these herbs to support our female health. Carving out intentional time to connect with our womb can be a sacred way to heal emotional or physical trauma

---

## When

Always consult with your trusted healthcare provider before trying yoni steaming to ensure it is the right practice for your female health 

Safe yoni steaming has specifics, it is important to NOT steam if you are:

- Pregnant
- Have an IUD
- Ovulating and trying to conceive
- Menstruating
- Have open sores or blisters on your yoni
- Have an active internal yoni infection with a fever

---

## Frequency

This is your practice depending on your specific needs

- **Once a month:** general wellness and detoxification
- **Twice a month:** postpartum healing, infertility, or painful periods
- **Three times a week:** clear infections or discomfort

---

## What you'll need

- Sacred time (30 minutes or so) 
- Yoni herbs (¼ cup per steam session)
- Glass, ceramic or steel bowl
- Water
- Yoni seat
- Skirt or dress long enough to cover your yoni seat and the floor to encase the steam
- Candles/lighter (optional)
- Mirror (optional)

Create your space - dim the lights, light candles, put on your favorite music; whatever zens you out to relax into

---

## How to steam

1. Bring 8 cups of purified hot water to a boil
2. Put your skirt on
3. Place your herbs into your bowl while thanking them for their support to your body
4. Pour the water over the herbs and stir
5. Situate your yoni seat over the bowl
6. Set a timer and sit on your seat with your skirt fully covering the seat - encase any heat/steam that could escape 
7. Add more hot water as needed
8. Meditate, read, sit in quiet gratitude but most importantly be present - check in & listen to your body. Is she enjoying this? Is the water too hot? How much time feels sufficient for you?
