## The Story of Me

With a nursing background in cardiac, neurology and emergency medicine, I have spent much of my time ingrained and trained in the traditional healthcare system. 
There has always been this sense of inescapable sickness among the patients I am caring for. 
It's an energy that never really had to be spoken but was always present… "How did my body get to this point? This isn't what I wanted for myself." 
I've found the sick care model is a deep rooted problem with a complicated way out.

As time moves forward, I've sat closely with how I'm living my life's purpose. 
I've learned that my heart is always with nursing but my spirit isn't with the traditional medicine framework.
I've learned that my purpose is to no longer engage with a faceless system but to make a real difference in 1 to 1 interactions with my clients instead.

I know that the care I give, infused through my knowledge and given from human to human is what truly makes a difference. 
I've really had to get clear with how I wanted to express my gifts as a healer. 

So *what and where* within the giving sphere can I call home? Naturally, the pivot has been to herbalism (remember making mud pies in the front yard as a child?)..
 It's been something that has felt gentle, intentional, accessible, familiar. 

My purpose in this life is to support my community in true health. To give my gift of my knowledge of medicine - lovingly and healthfully - from my soul. The coaching & products of the Sacred Portal Wellness are created with this intention in mind.

With love,
From Me to the health of You
