My Journey to Sacred Portal

My path to creating Sacred Portal began in the hospital, where I worked as a Registered Nurse. While I deeply valued my nursing education and the lives I touched, I felt a growing disconnect between the conventional medical model and the holistic care I knew women's bodies needed.

After experiencing my own hormonal challenges and finding limited support within the traditional healthcare system, I discovered yoni steaming. This ancient practice, combined with my nursing knowledge, opened a new path forward.

I pursued certification as a Holistic Nurse Coach, deepening my understanding of integrative wellness approaches. Sacred Portal was born from this journey—a space where traditional wisdom meets modern nursing care, where women can find both evidence-based support and sacred healing practices.

My Approach

I believe in the power of blending traditional wisdom with modern medical knowledge. My nursing background ensures that all recommendations are safe, evidence-based, and tailored to your unique health situation. My holistic training allows me to see beyond symptoms to support your whole being.

  • Empowerment: Education and tools to become your own best advocate
  • Compassion: A judgment-free space for your healing journey
