About This Blend

The Hormonal Harmony Blend is thoughtfully formulated to support women experiencing hormonal imbalances, irregular cycles, or PMS symptoms. This gentle yet effective blend combines traditional herbs known for their hormone-balancing properties.

Each ingredient is carefully selected for its specific benefits and works synergistically to support your body's natural hormonal rhythms.

Ingredients

  • Red Raspberry Leaf: Tones the uterus and supports menstrual health
  • Nettle: Rich in minerals and supports overall reproductive wellness
  • Vitex (Chasteberry): Balances hormones and regulates cycles
  • Rose Petals: Cooling and soothing for the reproductive system
  • Lavender: Calming and stress-reducing properties

All organic, sustainably sourced herbs.

How to Use

  1. Bring 4-6 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add 1/4 cup of herbal blend to the water
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes
  4. Remove from heat and let cool to a comfortable temperature
  5. Pour into your yoni steam seat or bowl
  6. Steam for 20-30 minutes, 1-2 times per week

Note: Do not steam during menstruation or pregnancy. Consult with a healthcare provider if you have any concerns.
