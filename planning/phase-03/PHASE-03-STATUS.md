## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigations

### Risk: Scope Creep in Visual Design
**Mitigation:** Enforce design freeze before development. Stick to documented specifications.

### Risk: Font Licensing Issues
**Mitigation:** Using free Google Fonts for initial launch. Custom fonts optional for future.

### Risk: Accessibility Gaps
**Mitigation:** Comprehensive accessibility guidelines documented. Validation required before approval.

### Risk: Timeline Delays
**Mitigation:** Clear specifications allow parallel work. Design team can execute efficiently.
