## Brand Voice & Tone

### Keywords
- Earthy
- Grounded
- Feminine
- Mossy forest witch
- Matriarchal medicine
- Trustworthy
- Wellness

### Voice Characteristics
- **Warm but professional:** Balance approachability with expertise
- **Educational:** Share knowledge without being preachy
- **Empowering:** Help women feel confident in their wellness journey
- **Authentic:** Speak from experience and genuine care

### Tone Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use inclusive, welcoming language
- Explain benefits clearly and honestly
- Share personal journey when relevant
- Honor traditional practices respectfully
- Maintain professional health boundaries

**Don't:**
- Make unverifiable medical claims
- Use overly clinical or cold language
- Oversell or use aggressive marketing
- Appropriate cultural practices without context
- Shame or judge different wellness choices
